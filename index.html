Welcome to the Druggy Dino NFT Project

Druggy Dino is an exciting new NFT project featuring a collection of unique, hand-drawn dinosaurs with quirky personalities. Each Druggy Dino is a one-of-a-kind digital art piece, stored on the Ethereum blockchain as an NFT (non-fungible token).

How to Buy a Druggy Dino NFT

  1. Download MetaMask

    First, download and install the MetaMask browser extension from the official MetaMask website. This will serve as your digital wallet for buying and storing Druggy Dino NFTs.

  2. Fund Your Wallet with Ethereum (ETH)

    Next, you need to fund your MetaMask wallet with Ethereum (ETH). You can purchase ETH from popular cryptocurrency exchanges like Coinbase or Binance. After purchasing ETH, transfer the desired amount to your MetaMask wallet address.

  3. Visit Our Mint Page

    Once your MetaMask wallet is funded with ETH, navigate to the Druggy Dino NFT mint page by clicking the "Mint" link in the navigation menu or by visiting (Coming Soon).

  4. Mint a Druggy Dino NFT

    On the mint page, connect your MetaMask wallet by clicking the "Connect Wallet" button. Once connected, choose the number of Druggy Dino NFTs you'd like to mint and click the "Mint" button. Confirm the transaction in your MetaMask wallet, and you will receive your new Druggy Dino NFT(s) shortly!
